# SUEWS
* Brief
  * Surface Urban Energy and Water Balance Scheme
  * urban radiaion – energy and water balance model. Includes LUMPS and can be used to calculate Q<sub>F</sub>.
  * Applicable Period: 5 min-hourly-annual
